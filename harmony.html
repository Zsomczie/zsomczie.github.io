Harmony in the Wild

Game System Design & Programming

About the Game

Harmony in the Wild is an open-world adventure where you play as a magical fox. You learn abilities from animal friends — gliding, swimming, smashing rocks, and more — as you uncover the story of a dying world brought back to life.

My Role

I worked on designing and programming the core game systems: movement mechanics, environmental interactions, and ability unlocking. A big focus was on seamless traversal and narrative integration.
